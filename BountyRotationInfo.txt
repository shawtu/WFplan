# Warframe Bounty Timers and Locations

## Bounties

### Cetus
- **Location**: Cetus  
- **Reputation**: Ostron  
- **Rotation**: A through C  
- **Timer**: 150 minutes  

### Fortuna
- **Location**: Orb Vallis (Fortuna)  
- **Reputation**: Solaris United  
- **Rotation**: A through C  
- **Timer**: 150 minutes  

### Necralisk
- **Location**: Cambion Drift (Necralisk)  
- **Reputation**: Entrati

### Zariman
